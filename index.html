About Our Leadership

"Our mission is not merely to protect assets — it is to architect the infrastructure of trust upon which the next generation of industry will be built."

— Alexander M. Vance, CEO

Founded on the principle that security and sustainability are complementary forces, Sentinel Corp has grown from a regional consultancy into one of the world's most trusted corporate infrastructure firms.

Under our leadership team's guidance, we've pioneered frameworks that integrate enterprise-grade cybersecurity with environmental governance, enabling clients across 180+ markets to operate with confidence and integrity.

Our approach combines deep technological expertise with a long-term commitment to the ecosystems — both digital and natural — that sustain our shared future.
